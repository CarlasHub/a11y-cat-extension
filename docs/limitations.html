Known limitations

The extension supports accessibility review workflows. It does not replace manual testing or assistive-technology validation.

Manual evidence still pending

  • Manual VoiceOver sign-off is still required.
  • Manual NVDA sign-off is still required.
  • Manual JAWS sign-off is still required.

Restricted pages and protected contexts

  • Restricted browser-managed pages are unsupported.
  • Protected or inaccessible iframes can limit scan coverage.
  • Cross-origin frames may not expose the evidence needed for full inspection.

File access and page scope limits

  • file:// pages are supported with limitations and require explicit browser permission.
  • Hidden, delayed, authenticated, or interactive states are not all exercised automatically.

Language and review limits

  • Spelling support is currently English-only in the bundled beta dictionary set.
  • Unsupported languages are skipped rather than being checked as English.
  • Review and advisory items are not confirmed failures until a person checks them in context.

Screen reader review limit

  • Virtual Screen Reader Review is not real NVDA, JAWS, or VoiceOver sign-off.
  • It is a local review aid, not a replacement for real assistive-technology testing.
Do not claim: full WCAG compliance, complete coverage, or complete AT validation from the extension alone.
